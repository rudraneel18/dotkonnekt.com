What we do

Technology



D2C Experiential Commerce

While Direct is the future of Commerce, D2C Brands constantly struggle to scale-up with the current monolithic & restrictive platform options. These jerry-rigged platforms were built for small entrepreneurs or large enterprises and clearly don’t meet the needs of a curated direct-to-consumer experience. Noticing this whitespace, we are building a full-stack Experiential Commerce platform, purpose-built for D2C. Let’s discuss how the power of content, community and commerce can help exponentially scale-up your brand.

Operations



Managed Commerce

D2C founders are passionate innovators who create hero product(s), build great communities and shape the future of our industry. Quite often, we see technology & operations becoming their Achilles’ heel as business scales up. We solve this with a Managed Commerce partnership for D2C brands – covering technology, engineering, web & marketplace, digital operations, customer experience and back-office support. Fully integrated and delivered to enable you to focus on what matters the most – launch innovative offerings, build deep customer relationships and grow your business.

Global Gateway

While launching a brand is now easier, scaling it has become quite a challenge. Emerging brands look for trusted partners to go global and cater to high-value customers in the larger markets. With our deep experience of operating & growing businesses globally, we are launching a Global gateway to help D2C brands launch & scale internationally, starting with US market. Be it local warehousing & fulfillment, production & supply chain, returns & customer experience, our partner ecosystem will help you access the biggest consumer market for brands.

Strategic Capital

Growing businesses with strong fundamentals need access to strategic capital, one that helps them not just raise funds, but get access to industry and market expertise, with some of the best industry executives as mentors to achieve the vision. dotkonnekt enables select D2C brands to grow exponentially with access to strategic investors, mentors and growth enablers.
